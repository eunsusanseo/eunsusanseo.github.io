Live Hive

Logo Design, 2015

Live Hive is a live video streaming application that allows users to broadcast and stream live events from their mobile devices. Use it to broadcast or stream press announcements, quick meetings, workshops or talks.


Logo Design

The main aim for the design was to represent the 'buzz' created when an audience forms, while presenting the app's main function of on-demand, live recording.
In addition, through the secondary honeycomb shape that the beehive is overlaid on, the logo can be scaled to function as an icon or hold the logo's title.


Research and Ideation

I started with a brainstorming phase by researching products with similar functions. This got my ideas flowing and helped brainstorm key words that the logo can embody.

Then, I made 'thought nodes' or 'idea trees' that stem from key words which represent the logo. Here, it's LIVE and STREAMING. From these two key nodes I start writing down other words that come to mind, mainly synonyms or even words that rhyme.


Through this, I gathered 2 things:

    (1) Key words that could be used to name the logo
    (2) Key design elements (shape, object, color) that will make up the logo.


In this case, the thought nodes were more useful for part (2) because the name was brainstormed at the project's onset.



Sketching

Finally to sketching! By this time, it was really helpful to have a name to base the logo on. The thought nodes came in handy because they got me thinking about possible design elements that could be used (e.g. the colors red and yellow, the beehive theme).


Revise & Repeat

In this phase, I think it's crucial to have a second (or third, or fourth) pair of eyes to look at the design. While I am sketching, a list of 'Questions to ask' or 'Opinions needed' often comes up. I am thankful to have had my design partner give her honest opinions, especially here.


Final Design

The first draft of the final logo is on the left. At the very end of this process, the other designer made a small edit to change the color of the words to create an even better design. #Teamwork!
